About

Our FTC Journey.

  • 2023

    Founding year

    The MCA Eagles were founded in 2023 by students passionate about robotics from the Edison Academy Magnet School in NJ for the Centerstage Season.

  • November 2023

    Rookie Season First Comp

    MCA Eagles win three matches in their first comp.

  • March 3, 2024

    Regional Competition

    In their rookie season regional meet, the MCA Eagles went 7-3, won the Innovate Award, and qualified for states!

  • March 17, 2024

    State Competition

    The team went 2-3 in the qualifiers and unfortunately did not qualify for worlds. However, the team's rookie season success has given the team confidence and excitement for its sophomore season!

Outreach

Read about a few of our impactful outreach initiatives to connect with our community!

Teaching lessons at Metuchen Public Library

We gave middle schoolers an opportunity to learn about the inner workings of our Centerstage Robot. We also taught them basic programming and mechanical engineering in robotics

Mentoring a FLL Team

We helped middle schoolers from North Brunswick kickstart their FLL team "Potato Power". We walked them through the processes of a FIRST robotics season. They were able to make states in their rookie year!
